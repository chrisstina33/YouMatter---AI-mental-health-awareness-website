1. Understand Your Rhythms 🧠

Your brain, heart, and body operate in natural frequencies, like a finely tuned orchestra.

System Frequency State
Brain (Delta) 0.5–4 Hz Deep sleep, repair
Brain (Theta) 4–8 Hz Relaxed, creative
Brain (Alpha) 8–12 Hz Calm focus, relief
Brain (Beta) 12–30 Hz Alert, problem-solving
Brain (Gamma) 30–100 Hz Learning, memory
Heart (HRV) 0.04–0.4 Hz Stress resilience
Respiration ~0.1 Hz Parasympathetic calm
